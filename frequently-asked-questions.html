Frequently Asked Questions
Why did you make a website?

Honestly, I've wanted a space to share my creations publicly for a while now. That being said, the decision to learn at least basic web development skills to make this site came after some words of encouragement from the lovely Izzymandias.

Do you regret your username?

Not yet, but it's coming.

Have you seen [insert movie/tv show]? / Have you read [insert book]?

Very probably not. I am hoping to change this and welcome recommendations, but I tend to consume media at a slower pace than most so don't expect anything from me soon after you've recommended something.

Can I join your Minecreaft Realm?

(Ages 18-26) If I already know you and you're interested, feel free to contact me about it. That said, Pladsylvania is in a bit of a slump at the moment as most of my time and energy is being directed elsewhere. For this reason, I am not accepting any strangers' requests to join.

(Under 18 / Over 26) No, unfortunately I'm not accepting new members outside of this age range, in the interest of ensuring a safe and comfortable community for our members.

Why is your realm on Bedrock? Why don't you switch to Java?

There is no 'correct' edition of Minecraft to play on, and quite frankly I'm tired of pretending there is. If you must know, I prefer Bedrock because in my experience it has better performance, accessibility and mechanics overall. No, I'm not interested in debating the topic, if you prefer to play on Java, you go do that.

What is your favourite colour?

Really? Take a guess. You can figure it out, I believe in you.

How can I contact you?

My DMs are open! Discord is undoubtedly the fastest way to reach me; Reddit works as well, but I don't check that nearly as frequently so expect a delayed response.
