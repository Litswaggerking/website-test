I intend for this space to be a repository of all my various musings and hijinks, but most prominently a place to display two project in particular. The first of these is the Pladsylvanian Confederation, a Minecraft Bedrock Edition realm I've been running since 2023. The latter is Orbital Isles, a personal worldbuilding project and the setting of my upcoming short story; To Walk Betwixt Stars.
